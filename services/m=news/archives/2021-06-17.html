Most of the time we are having back to back game update and it is fantastic! However, we are working on some updates for our website as well and I would love to share the progress with all of you.

So we have changed the layout of the website's nav-bar like the sneak preview last time. We have added all your basic needs to the drop down

The First section is Game Guide

  • Game Guide : The Game Guide is where you are going to find guides of ingame content and is the main hub of this section.
  • The Credit System : Since credits are such a big part of 2009scape. I thought that it would be nice to have it as a drop down option.
  • Our Rules : A new rule page to pair with the new Discord #rules section. This will help players who do not use discord to know the rules to avoid getting banned.
  • About 2009scape: A new about page with a little bit of history on us with our goals in mind.

The next section is the Community Garden ( You can click on the tab itself to go to the garden. )
  • Discord Server : A link to our discord if you could not find the giant one on the home page.
  • Matrix Server : A link to our matrix community which was hidden before.
  • The Highscore pages still where they have been and will not be changed.
  • The Hall of Fame: For now it links to the legacy HoF but it will be updated to link to a hub.
  • Meet the Staff : Now you can know who is who within the staff.
  • To the Tributes : A page dedicated to our contributors who aren't staff as a thank you.
  • Play Classic : The og Rs 2009 site actually had a Classic page and this one links to a classic page that showcases Open RSC ( Open source Runescape Classic )

The last section is the Help Center and much ike the Community Garden just click the tab to go to the hub.
  • Frequently Asked Questions: This is a new page based off of the discord's FAQ but also has a few that are not on Discord. Make sure to read this.
  • Bug report section : Make sure to do Super detailed bug reports with all sorts of information, coords, rs wiki link for check, etc. More information will be over there.

The update page was also updated to look more like a forums and the home page is currently underconstruction.

More pages are on their way but this is what we currently have ready!
